# Week 01 - I like colors

## 🎯 Sentence Pattern
**I like [color].**

## 🎨 Vocabulary
- red
- blue
- yellow
- green
- pink

## 🗣️ Example Sentences
- I like red.
- I like blue.
- I like yellow.

## 🧩 Game Idea
🎯 找颜色游戏：家人轮流找出房间里的指定颜色，说“I like ___”。
